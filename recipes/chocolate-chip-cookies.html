Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

I think this recipe originally came off the back of a bag of Nestle chocolate chips. I like it because it uses shortening rather than butter which I think gives a better texture. And it works with the off-brand chocolate chips :)

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4C flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 C shortening
  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4C brown sugar
  • 3/4C white sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 8-12oz chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Mix dry ingredients (first 3) in one bowl and wet ingredients (next 5) in another.
  3. Add chocolate chips to wet mixture, then combine wet and dry ingredients
  4. Shape dough into 1 in balls and bake for approximately 13 min. Makes 2 1/2-3 dozen cookies.
